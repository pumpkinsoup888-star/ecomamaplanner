Our mission

Helping mamas plan smarter,
live greener

We created EcoMamaPlanner because we believe that preparing for a baby shouldn't mean drowning in information, overspending on things you don't need, or adding to the planet's waste problem.

Our free tools give every expecting mama — regardless of budget or background — access to a personalized, eco-conscious pregnancy plan that covers everything from week 1 to the day baby arrives.

Why we built
EcoMamaPlanner

Pregnancy is one of the most exciting — and overwhelming — experiences of a woman's life. Between doctor appointments, baby shower registries, nursery prep, and endless Google searches, it's easy to lose sight of what actually matters.

We built EcoMamaPlanner to cut through the noise. One website. One plan. Everything an eco-conscious mama needs to feel prepared, informed, and calm — from the first positive test to the hospital bag.

We also believe that choosing eco-friendly options shouldn't be complicated or expensive. Our guides make it simple to make greener choices that save money long-term — like cloth nappies, solar-powered nursery products, and natural baby skincare.

Common questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is EcoMamaPlanner really free?

Yes — completely free. There is no sign-up, no account, no credit card, and no premium tier required to generate and download your full personalized pregnancy plan. We are supported by Google AdSense advertising and Amazon affiliate links, which allow us to keep the planner free for everyone.

Is my personal information stored?

No. Your name, due date, and preferences are entered directly in your browser, used to generate your plan, and never transmitted to or stored on any server. Your plan is generated entirely on your device. We take your privacy seriously — see our full Privacy Policy for details.

Is the information on EcoMamaPlanner medically accurate?

We strive to provide accurate, evidence-based pregnancy information, but we are not medical professionals. All content on EcoMamaPlanner is for general educational purposes only and should not replace advice from your doctor, midwife, or healthcare provider. Always consult a qualified medical professional for personal health decisions during pregnancy.

What does the pregnancy plan include?

Your personalized plan includes: your current week and trimester overview, baby development milestones, body changes, trimester-appropriate nutrition guidance, safe exercise recommendations, mental wellness tips, warning signs to know, eco-friendly product recommendations, a solar nursery guide, a baby budget tracker, and a hospital bag checklist. All personalised to your due date and preferences.

Do I need to be eco-conscious to use EcoMamaPlanner?

Absolutely not. Our planner and blog are designed to be welcoming and useful for all expecting mamas — whether you're deeply committed to sustainability or simply curious about making more natural choices during pregnancy. We believe in progress over perfection, and every small step toward a healthier, more mindful pregnancy is worth celebrating.

How can I contact EcoMamaPlanner?

We'd love to hear from you! Whether you have a question, a suggestion, a partnership inquiry, or just want to share your story, please reach out through our contact page. We aim to respond within 24-48 hours.
